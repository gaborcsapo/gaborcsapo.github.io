About me

Hi! I’m Gabor a Computer Science major studying at New York University Abu Dhabi. I’m from Hungary, but thanks to my studies I lived for two years in the Middle East, for one semester in Shanghai, China and soon I’ll be moving to New York. I didn’t always know that I wanted to be a Computer Scientist, but in my freshman year I realized that my passion lies in creation and building. Since then I’ve learnt a great deal about designing efficient software in class and in my free-time. I’ve had an internships in Africa and in Germany, while I also work as a freelancer mainly making websites. I’m always out for an adventure, I love hiking, surfing and wakeboarding. I’m grateful to NYUAD for giving me the opportunity to travel to 15+ countries in the past 3 years of which I tried to make the best use of by learning about different cultures and life in many places.

Side Projects

DIYDictionary

This is a selfish project. When I moved a foreign language environment, I met countless new words that I wanted to learn myself. First, I wrote them on pieces of papers, but I kept losing them. I wished there had been an app to store the words electronically… Then I realized I’m a CS major, why not make one for myself?!? I was introduced at a Hackathon in Shanghai to Hybrid Apps. To try them out I used the Ionic Framework with AngularJS and SQLite to accomplish the task I set myself. I have a huge list of plans how to develop the project into a commercially available app.

Game Design

I like to have fun while I’m learning and the easiest way to do so is to design games. My very first program was a copy of the Snake game that I used to play on my Dad’s Nokia. I also created a 2D Creativity Puzzle Game, that requires some serious brain power and the demolishment of mental walls to solve. I created that project to learn about Processing and animations. In my Software Engineering class we focused on software design patterns. We learnt about the Factory, Singleton, MVC, Decorator and Observer patterns by creating board and card games.

Visual Design

Graphic design is a crucial part of Software Development. People judge in 3 seconds whether to download an app, or whether to look at that blog post or not. We have to acknowledge that humans are visual mammals judging by the looks. It is important to have an eye for pretty design s to please every audience. I contributed to logo designs, printout designs and a marketing campaign for the Burj Khalifa in Dubai. My style for the web is the trendy minimalist design but I always want to spice it up with some unique ideas, which is true for most of the things I do. I often get my inspiration from the great artists of Bauhaus like Laszlo Moholy-Nagy.
